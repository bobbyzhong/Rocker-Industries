The Rocker Advantage

Dual Solutions, Detailed Analysis, and Rapid Prototyping

We Listen

Rocker Industries understands diverse solenoid requirements, based on various strokes, forces, pressures, and durabilities. The range of solenoids that Rocker Industries produces is limited only by the range of our customer’s requirements.

We Respond

Whether we design a custom solenoid, or precisely manufacture a solenoid to a customer’s existing detailed drawings and specifications, Rocker Industries’ solenoids meet performance requirements for all aerospace, defense and commercial applications.

We Deliver

Rocker Industries delivers a full spectrum of single or multiple coil solenoids, mechanical ball latching solenoids, pressurized or non-pressurized solenoids, current-switching solenoids and rectified VAC solenoids.

The Basics:

Rocker Industries' Solenoid Valve Types

Rocker Industries produces all standard solenoid valve types, including: butterfly, cartridge, co-axial, in-line, latching, pilot operated, poppet, two-way, three-way and four-port solenoid valve designs.

Rocker Industries designs and manufactures hydraulic, fuel, and pneumatic solenoid valves that meet simplicity and efficiency requirements, while flexibly responding to customer needs for direct or pilot-operation, and incorporating variable voltage, flow, and pressure requirements.

Beyond the Basics:

Rocker Industries' Solenoid Valve Options

Rocker Industries also offers options to meet customer requirements for solenoid valves, including various poppet designs, multiple coil redundancies, variable latching options, and normally open-close configurations.

Mission Critical:

Your Solenoid Valve Requirements

Rocker Industries understands each application has its own set of mission critical requirements. For some applications, durability, reliable repeatability, and quick response time matter most.

Rocker Industries works closely with each customer to match the best solenoid valve type and options with each application’s specific requirements to achieve optimal operating results.
