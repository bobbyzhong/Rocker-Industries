The Rocker Advantage

Dual Solutions, Detailed Analysis & Rapid Prototyping

Dual Solutions

Rocker Industries can take customer's new solenoid and solenoid valve requirements, including source control drawings, and deliver full custom design, analysis, prototyping, and final production. We can also take customer’s existing detail design drawings, processes and specifications, and deliver precise production.This dual solutions approach delivers a full range of solenoids and integrated electro-mechanical solenoid valves.

Engineering Excellence

Rocker’s experienced engineers employ state-of-the-art SolidWorks CAD, and FEA with 2D and 3D static and dynamic solvers. Detailed engineering analysis and reports are available for custom specification projects in order to provide added support during you design reviews including Magnetic FEA, Vibration Analysis, Heat Transfer and Fluid Analysis, Concept Design from customer specification and more.

Traditions & Quality Assurance

Since 1954, Rocker Industries has been designing and manufacturing the highest quality solenoids and solenoid valves for the aerospace, defense and commercial industries, at a competitive price. Our Quality Assurance System meets out industries' highest standards. We are certified by Det Norske Vertias (DNV) to AS9100 and ISO9001
