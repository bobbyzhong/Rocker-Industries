Quality

Rocker Industries is a Certified Dock-to-Stock supplier for the majority of our customers. We strive to find new ways to continually improve our product and processes, including implementation of a certified operator program throughout our facility.

The Quality Assurance System at Rocker Industries meets our industries’ highest requirements. Rocker Industries is certified by Det Norske Vertias (DNV) to AS9100 and ISO9001, and has certified in-house testing facilities to perform all necessary testing, including: proof pressure, flow rate, pressure drop, force, thermal, environmental, response time, life cycle and electrical check.
